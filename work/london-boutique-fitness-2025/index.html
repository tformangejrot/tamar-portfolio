What is this report?

London has been a leader in boutique fitness among European capitals. This report provides an overview of the London boutique fitness market, the evolution of different modalities, and their geographic distribution. It is based on a large representative data set. Exact studio counts are not fully accurate but relative percentages and growth numbers capture real insights.
