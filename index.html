About

Hello, I'm Tamar

I'm a former studio founder with a background in boutique fitness, EdTech, operations, and product. I've launched two pole dance studios (Boston & Paris), built teams and systems from scratch, and learned how to do a whole lot with very little.

I love being the person who brings clarity, momentum, and structure to fast-moving teams—whether that's through special projects, strategic ops, or just getting stuff done. I'm currently exploring how AI tools can support business analysis, ops, and customer experience, and I'm always up for combining technical curiosity with human-centered strategy.

On my page, you can find links to my Substack, trends analyses, and more as I add and develop new work.
