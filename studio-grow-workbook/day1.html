FOUR BUYER TYPES

👤
Spontaneous
Spontane
Spontaneous buyers make quick decisions. They respond to urgency and immediate value. Your communication should be direct, action-oriented, and create a sense of "now or never."
👓
Methodical
Methodical (men)
Methodical buyers need information, data, and time to process. They want to understand the logic and see proof. Provide detailed FAQs, comparisons, and let them research.
💬
Humanistic
Humanistic/story (women)
Humanistic buyers connect through stories, emotions, and relationships. Share member success stories, community values, and personal connections. They want to feel part of something meaningful.
🏆
Competitive
Competitive (growing w/ pandemic)
Competitive buyers want to win, achieve goals, and see results. Emphasize challenges, progress tracking, achievement, and how your studio helps them outperform.
